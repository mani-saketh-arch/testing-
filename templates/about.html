Emergency Procedures

Call 911 immediately for:
  • Chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Unconsciousness or severe confusion
  • Severe bleeding or trauma
  • Signs of stroke (face drooping, arm weakness, speech difficulty)

SafeIndy can guide you through basic first aid while emergency services are en route.

In case of fire:
  1. Call 911 immediately
  2. Get out and stay out - never go back inside
  3. If trapped, signal for help from a window
  4. Meet firefighters at a safe distance

SafeIndy can help you locate the nearest fire station and provide evacuation guidance.

Tornado Warning:
  • Go to the lowest floor, center room, away from windows
  • Cover yourself with blankets or mattress
  • Stay informed with weather radio or SafeIndy updates

SafeIndy provides real-time weather alerts and can guide you to the nearest shelter.
